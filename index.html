Our Philosophy

A Place to Grow, Explore & Belong

We offer a fun, safe, nurturing and caring environment in which children are encouraged to develop their potential. Staff and parents work together to offer a stimulating setting for children to increase their sense of self-worth, with the following results:

🤝

Trust & Connection

Our children will learn to trust the teacher, other adults and other children.

🌟

Independence

They will foster independence through being able to choose their own activities.

👐

Cooperation

They will learn cooperation through sharing, waiting one's turn and being part of a group.

🎨

Creative Materials

They will learn to use materials properly and creatively.

🎓

School Readiness

They will gain the skills required to succeed in their future education.

🌿

Non-Profit & Parent-Led

A true cooperative — parents and teachers working together for every child's benefit.

Our Home

A Community Space Since 1970

Rockwood Nursery School has called the historic Rockwood Town Hall home for over 50 years. It's a bright, spacious, welcoming space — right in the heart of the community at 191 Main St S.

As a parent-led, non-profit cooperative, we keep our fees among the most affordable in the region. Parents play an active role when they volunteer as part of one of the many committees that make our school run, which is what makes our low monthly fees possible.

💰 CWELCC — Canada-Wide Early Learning & Child Care

Rockwood Nursery School is a proud participant in the Canada-Wide Early Learning and Child Care (CWELCC) Program, a federal-provincial initiative working toward $10/day child care across Canada. This means even greater savings for eligible families enrolled in our programs.

What is a Registered ECE?

A Registered Early Childhood Educator (RECE) is provincially licensed by the College of Early Childhood Educators of Ontario — the regulatory body that sets professional standards for child care in Ontario. RECEs have completed post-secondary education in child development, hold a valid licence, and follow a strict Code of Ethics. Having RECEs in the classroom means your child is in the most qualified, accountable hands possible.
