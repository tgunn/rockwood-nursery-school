What is a Registered ECE?

A Registered Early Childhood Educator (RECE) is provincially licensed by the College of Early Childhood Educators of Ontario — the regulatory body that sets professional standards for child care in Ontario. RECEs have completed post-secondary education in child development, hold a valid licence, and follow a strict Code of Ethics. Having RECEs in the classroom means your child is in the most qualified, accountable hands possible.
